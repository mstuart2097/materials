Lab #6: it’s shiny!

Lab Assignment

The objective of today’s lab is to create a shiny web-app.

There are no restrictions in terms of the topic.

Minimum specs for your app: use a data set, have at least three (meaningful) widgets, and at least one chart. At least one of the widgets is supposed to be implemented from the chart (i.e. you’ll need to use ggvis, plotly or d3scatter)

Deliverable

Submit your file app.R and all of the data needed to Blackboard.
